ORIGINS

From the beginning of time, an omnipotent being lacking physical form roamed throughout the galaxies, searching for a way to quench his thirst for conquest. As eons passed, the entity felt himself distancing from his ultimatum -- until he stumbled upon universe RT 57-0935. Within the constraints of the universe, the being had trouble finding any signs of worthy territory to pursue his journey. However, through a stroke of good fortune, the traveler was able to discover a barren planet with one distinction from the trillions of others: the planet was inundated with a liquid substance capable of spawning life. Upon his discovery, the being immediately began to channel his energy into spawning a variety of different creatures. His goal was to populate the planet, meticulously crafting an army of beings that would be distinguishable through the various physical features he assigned to them. Once the finishing touches were added, the army was given a single order: to conquer the universe. That is the last that was known of the deity and his army... but legend has it that they arrived in the Milky Way galaxy, straight to a location three leaps from the sun, in order to conquer planet Earth through the screens that enchant the many souls inhabiting it...
