Help people in Ukraine together with DIM

The DIM project is designed to provide homeowners with prompt and complete information about the condition of their homes, despite distance and war.

We help change the world for the better
Why this service should exist?

Due to the war in Ukraine, millions of people were forced to leave their homes and travel far from their hometown or country.

The decision to return home often becomes risky, because the condition of the house can only be seen by returning to the house yourself.

That is why we decided to collect data on the condition of houses in the territories affected by military actions, so that every home owner knows the condition of his own home and can check updated statuses.

How it works
Register your organization
We welcome among our partners organizations that have the desire and opportunity to help people, who left their homes, to know the statutes of their settlements.
01
Invite volunteers to your team
Do you have a team that is open to new important challenges? Offer volunteers to join the initiative.
02
Volunteers get address requests
Aid workers should be ready to check the condition of houses and apartments in settlements affected by the war.
03
Volunteers fill information about verified homes
Aid workers checking the status of abandoned homes will fill in such data as: security, electricity, clean water, gas supply, road to the home, working local hospitals.

Report statuses

Fill in information about the status of the house and the surrounding area, the state of electricity, water and gas supply, working hospitals nearby, etc. Any of this information is extremely important to homeowners.
