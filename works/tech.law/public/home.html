WHAT WE ARE EXPERT AT

Legal Practice Areas
CONTRACT LAW
We specialize in drafting, reviewing and negotiating commercial contracts and agreements in the global arena. Our international team assist clients in the UK, Europe, Saudi Arabia, Dubai, Australia and the US. We are also able to assist international government achieve their goals, including large projects and initiatives.
DATA PROTECTION LAW
With the protection of personal data recognised as a fundamental human right following the implementation of the General Data Protection Regulation, and multiple jurisdictions following suit, we help our clients navigate through the regulation and ensure that they have the necessary contracts, processes and procedures in place to avoid any non-compliance.
GOVERNANCE
Our experts are able to provide our clients with compliant policies and processes, as well as monitoring techniques to monitor ongoing governance and compliance. We are also able to support our clients with governance of the Company Secretariat functions, managing key stakeholders and ensuing that records are accurately presented and recorded.
